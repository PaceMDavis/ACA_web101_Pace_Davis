Catering

Caffeinate with Georgia

Elevate your special event with Georgia Coffee catering. A homegrown Austin original, we offer locally-roasted, fair trade organic coffee from artisanal roaster Texas Coffee Traders. Our catering menu features premium air-roasted hot coffee, our renowned cold brew coffee, and our sweet and creamy Iced Georgia.

From weddings to corporate gatherings, our catering service provides freshly brewed caffeinated beverages delivered directly to your event. Our friendly, experienced staff will set up coffee self-service for groups of 25 to 1,000 in the Austin area. We love working with our clients to create a tailored coffee experience that offers highest-quality coffee and a no-fuss vendor experience.

Weddings | Corporate Gatherings | Conferences | Special Events | Education | Holiday Parties

Catering Menu

Our catering menu offers a self-serve “coffee bar” featuring our most popular coffee drinks. All of our coffee is made from ethically-sourced, organic beans.

Georgia House Blend Coffee – Created exclusively at Texas Coffee Traders, our organic house coffee offers a medium-dark roast featuring warm, chocolaty flavors of South America.

  • 5 GALLONS (100 PEOPLE) $100.00
  • 2.5 GALLONS (50 PEOPLE) $50.00

Georgia Cold Brew– Our acclaimed cold brew recipe is brewed in-house for 16 hours with South American beans specially roasted by Texas Coffee Traders. Our cold brew offers a highly caffeinated coffee drink with smooth, deep chocolatey flavors.

  • 5 GALLONS (80 PEOPLE) $250.00
  • 2.5 GALLONS (40 PEOPLE) $125.00

Iced Georgia – A customer favorite, Iced Bennu is a sweet and creamy iced coffee drink made with cold brewed Texas pecan coffee.

  • 5 GALLONS (80 PEOPLE) $300.00
  • 2.5 GALLONS (40 PEOPLE) $150.00
